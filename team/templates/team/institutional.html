The Sports League is the organising body of the Sports League with responsibility for the competition, its Rule Book and the centralised broadcast and other commercial rights.

WHO WE ARE

The Sports League is an Proffesional Football League for men's association Football. The Sports League is a corporation in which the 8 member clubs act as shareholders. In this league each member club has to play against all other member clubs twice.One match will played in the member club's home match and the other will be an away match There will be a total of 112 matches per season

It is going to be the 12th season in Sports League.Last year FC barcelona won the Sports League.Real madrid C.F holds the record for winning most number of titles
